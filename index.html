About Incubation Center, NIT Patna

About us

Government of Bihar unveiled the Bihar & Start Policy 2017 to foster entrepreneurship and selected NIT Patna as one of the pilot institutes.

  • NIT Patna established the Incubation Center in order to collaborate with the government of bihar to nurture entrepreneurship.
  • The Incubation Center facilitates networking with professional resources, including mentors, experts, investors, industry professionals, consultants and advisors.
  • Many technologies/innovations which have potential for commercial ventures have been identified b the Incubation Center, NIT Patna.
  • Interested in learning more about Incubation Center NIT Patna ? We invite you to keep exploring our website, and get in touch with questions about our values, community and more. Join us today!

Our Mission

A support system

To create physical infrastructure and support system necessary for business incubation activities.

Promote Professionalism

To facilitate practices with professional resources, which include mentors, experts and consultant advisors for the incubated companies.

Resourcefulness

To provide links to higher education resources.

Our Vision

Nurture Entrepreneurship

To nurture entrepreneurship in those people who have a passion for creating something of their own which can benefit the society.

Creating an Ecosystem

Creating an ecosystem in which people can get out of their comfort zone and work on new ideas, technology to solve existing problems.

Help Bihar

Make Bihar a hub of entrepreneurship and entrepreneurs.

Incubations

Incubations at NIT Patna

NIT Patna established the Incubation Center to create a start-up ecosystem not only in college but in the whole state.

  • The institute will provide all basic infrastructural support,ie,office space, meeting room, platform to do networking, management assistance and other support services.
  • Currently 3 start ups have been incubated and funded
  • Onibus
  • Busy Mechanic
  • Shekhar Telesystems
  • Redtron EducationService LLP

Onibus

Onibus is a service-based company (app and web-based platform) that provides a smarter way of communication between school and parents regarding all key activities of their students. Onibus is a platform that brings technology to schools and helps the parents to stay updated with their child’s curriculum.

Busy Mechanic

Busy Mechanic is a group of talented mobile mechanics who will offer you reliable vehicle care and service at the best price at your house or office as per your time schedule and convenience.
